Plain-language guide

A team of AI agents, not one know-it-all

A single AI assistant tries to do everything at once: find the facts, write the answer, and check its own work. It is fast, but it rarely catches its own mistakes. A multi-agent system splits that job across a small team of specialists who hand work to each other, just like people in an office.

One agent

Researches, writes, and judges in a single step. No second opinion. If it gets a fact wrong, the wrong fact goes straight into the answer.

A team of agents

A researcher gathers facts, a writer shapes them, and a critic checks the result and can send it back. Each agent does one job well.
